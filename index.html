How it works

Log your entries

You can introduce your income and expenses using an entry form with four fields.

Income defines money received, especially regularly. Income entries are represented with green color in the entries table.

An expense is an amount of money needed or used to do or buy something.

Your entries will appear as a list, with the most recent input on top.

Description is a short report of your income/expenses.

Category Income is required to be your first category, money received. All other categories represent expenses.

Date past and current date.

Amount whole and two decimal places numbers, using comma character.

Dashboard

Balance displays current balance, a sum of incomes, and subtraction of expenses.

Currency allows you to select your preferred currency type. You can choose just one option, another option can be made after pressing reset.

Weekly balance shows expenses you made in a week's time (Monday-Sunday).

Monthly balance shows your monthly payments.

Set limit are your chosen limits for weekly, respectively, monthly expenses. When these limits are exceeded, weekly and monthly balance will be updated with red color.
